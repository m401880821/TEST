- 在給定的變異數水準下選擇預期報酬率最高的投資組合，或在給定的預期報酬率下選擇變異數最低的投資組合。 [\[24\]](https://app.heptabase.com/4a1a2ab0-5382-4818-b264-b2de6aa2ff3d/card/67368656-ce58-40d4-a441-7c60bf6bfa54?pdfPage=5&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A30.959999999999997%2C%22top%22%3A210.6%2C%22width%22%3A657.3599999999999%2C%22height%22%3A85.32000000000002%7D)

**第 20 頁：一個風險資產與一個無風險資產：示例 1**

- 假設：$E(r_P) = 10\%$, $\sigma_P = 22\%$, $r_f = 2\%$。 [\[93\]](https://app.heptabase.com/4a1a2ab0-5382-4818-b264-b2de6aa2ff3d/card/67368656-ce58-40d4-a441-7c60bf6bfa54?pdfPage=20&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A30.959999999999997%2C%22top%22%3A14.58%2C%22width%22%3A610.56%2C%22height%22%3A70.2%7D)[\[94\]](https://app.heptabase.com/4a1a2ab0-5382-4818-b264-b2de6aa2ff3d/card/67368656-ce58-40d4-a441-7c60bf6bfa54?pdfPage=20&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A268.56%2C%22top%22%3A99.9%2C%22width%22%3A125.28000000000003%2C%22height%22%3A108%7D)

- **完整投資組合的預期報酬：** [\[95\]](https://app.heptabase.com/4a1a2ab0-5382-4818-b264-b2de6aa2ff3d/card/67368656-ce58-40d4-a441-7c60bf6bfa54?pdfPage=20&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A32.4%2C%22top%22%3A221.94%2C%22width%22%3A475.92%2C%22height%22%3A27.54000000000002%7D)

   $$
   \begin{aligned} E(r_C) &= y \times E(r_P) + (1 - y) \times r_f \\ &= r_f + y \times [E(r_P) - r_f] \\ &= 0.02 + y \times 0.08 \end{aligned}
   $$

   [\[96\]](https://app.heptabase.com/4a1a2ab0-5382-4818-b264-b2de6aa2ff3d/card/67368656-ce58-40d4-a441-7c60bf6bfa54?pdfPage=20&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A176.4%2C%22top%22%3A267.84%2C%22width%22%3A365.0400000000001%2C%22height%22%3A70.74000000000001%7D)

- **完整投資組合的風險（標準差）：** [\[97\]](https://app.heptabase.com/4a1a2ab0-5382-4818-b264-b2de6aa2ff3d/card/67368656-ce58-40d4-a441-7c60bf6bfa54?pdfPage=20&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A32.4%2C%22top%22%3A363.96000000000004%2C%22width%22%3A345.6%2C%22height%22%3A27.539999999999964%7D)

   $$
   \sigma_C = y \times \sigma_P = 0.22 \times y
   $$
